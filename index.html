Season Highlights

The Gilmour Academy Lancers softball team had a standout 2024–25 season, finishing with an impressive 15–4 record and showing consistent growth and resilience throughout the year. The team dominated at home, posting a long winning streak that highlighted their cohesion and determination. They pulled off several exciting victories, including a tight extra-inning win over North Royalton and strong performances against tough Division I opponents. Overall, the Lancers demonstrated balance and consistency, combining solid pitching, timely hitting, and smart defense to carry momentum into the postseason. Their season reflected steady progress and established the program as a rising force in the region.

Player Spotlights

During the 2024–25 season, Ava Gundling and Camden Dreiling were standout contributors for the Gilmour Academy Lancers softball team, combining elite offensive and defensive production. Ava posted a phenomenal batting average of .647 with 11 doubles, 3 triples, 10 home runs, and 25 RBIs over 17 games, showcasing her power and consistency at the plate. Camden complemented her performance both as a pitcher and hitter, throwing 175.2 innings with a 3.19 ERA, 20 wins, and 148 strikeouts, while also hitting .500 with 29 hits and 15 RBIs in 18 games. Together, their contributions fueled the Lancers’ impressive 15–4 record, providing leadership, versatility, and clutch performance that helped carry the team through a strong regular season and into the postseason.

Upcoming Events

The Gilmour Academy Lancers softball team’s schedule for the 2025–26 season has not yet been released, but there are reports that the coaching staff is planning a challenging slate of games.
